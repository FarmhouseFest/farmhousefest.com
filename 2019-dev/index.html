Farmhouse Fest is a celebration of farmhouse-style beers and ciders. Dry and refreshing to funky and complex, these are products made with a passion for the highest-quality ingredients and a no-shortcuts approach given the proper time to mature and evolve into something wonderful.

Set on the idyllic working farm at UBC, the atmosphere is just as important as the pours. Whether relaxing on a picnic blanket or catching some shade under the big tent, our spacious natural setting offers unique experiences for a festival of this nature.
