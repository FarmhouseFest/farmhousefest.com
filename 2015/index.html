Something Witty About What We're Doing

In addition to being BC's first themed beer festival, Farmhouse Fest is committed to giving craft beer lovers a high quality experience, centering around a universal, shared love of the art. Farmhouse Fest will be held at the UBC Farm, concurrent with the UBC Farmer's Market, emphasizing the complementary relationship between local food and beer.
